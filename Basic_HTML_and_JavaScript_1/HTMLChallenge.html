Article Tags

This is an example of Article tags in HTML5, developed so that you can set aside specific self-contained information regarding different subjects, making it easier to identify and format.

Detail Tags

Detail tags specify additional details that the user can open or close, and gives an interactive widget that the user can control. It helps to both compact a website so that information that may not always be necessary is hidden away, and to give interactivity to your site, enhancing user engagement. The summary however is used to give a small tidbit of information regarding a topic that may be listed in the details tag.
